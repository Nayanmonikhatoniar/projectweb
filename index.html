About Animal Welfare People

Founded in 2015, Animal Welfare People is a registered NGO based in Dibrugarh, Assam dedicated to improving the lives of animals through rescue operations, medical care, and community education.

Our team of volunteers works tirelessly to respond to calls about animals in distress, provide emergency medical care, and find loving homes for abandoned pets.
